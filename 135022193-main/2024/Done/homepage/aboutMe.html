About Me

I am a 21 year old, (currently) self taught programmer from The Netherlands trying to find a career in software.

When I was 18 I graduated from Business Administrator and found a job in the field pretty fast.
However after a few months I already started noticing how I never felt fullfilled in my job and
I very quickly got to a point where learning every day just wasnt possible and my favorite thing to do is learn.

So I made the decision to quit and to go look for something that would make me happy.
I very quickly found an interest in programming, but with no clue what i was gonna do i decided
to just start and see where it would lead me. For the first year or so of my programming journey
I wasnt making much progress, it felt very difficult to learn and super overwhelming.

Feeling overwhelmed combined with the confusion of not being sure what my future would
look like it wasnt easy but I never gave up and kept trying. Eventually i found CS50,
the free Havard computer science crash course and realised i didnt just like the idea of programming,
i loved programming. It gave me a constant challenge to overcome and endless new things to learn
and the amazing feeling of finally fixing that problem has still not gone away.

Now finally about 2 years in, I signed up to a pretty special school in Amsterdam called Codam,
a 42 school to be exact. I still have to get through the 4 week Piscine(bootcamp) to get in,
but i have never wanted anything more in my life and i'll be sure to give it my all.
